Обучение в колледже дает возможность пойти обучаться дальше на магистра или в профессиональные школы(медицина, теология, юриспруденция). Also, after the sixth grade, there is an opportunity to go to a combined junior-senior high school. After this stage, the senior classes of secondary school come. Usually they study in high school for 5 years.  Then there is a choice to go to vocational schools or colleges. College education gives you the opportunity to go on to study for a master's degree or to professional schools (medicine, theology, law).  Master's degree studies take 2 years, doctoral studies take three or more years. Next comes the research paper.
